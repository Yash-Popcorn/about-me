current state of life

Oct 23, 2025 Fall

In a few years, I got to try out hackathon, research, and internship. I am incredibly grateful for the opportunities I've had. My intention of experimenting with new things at a young age was to have more clarity about what I want to spend my time on. However, I am actually not sure what I want to exactly do. It actually led to become more confused about the future. In a world where events always happen, adaptability is crucial and predicting the future becomes a bit more difficult. So, it feels like I am betting what will happen (my intuition I guess).

I guess you can only connect the dots backwards, and I wish I connect them forward (would be super cool lol).

I currently have this one idea related to an old hobby of mine. I recently talked with my Mom about maybe not doing an internship, but she desperately wants me to do one. She doesn't believe in my working solo on a project, but it makes sense because my mom is just narrating what her friend's kids usually do. I am not sure about an internship exactly yet because I am really passionate about this idea, so I am going to start working really hard on this idea.
